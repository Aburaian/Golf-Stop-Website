This is RUFF

RUFF Indoor Golf started grew out of common love and passion for golf. The vision was to fill a missing part of golf in Sweden. RUFF is a place where golf is centered, but in an environment where you can socialize with friends, family, colleagues, and business partners.

ABOUT RUFF

A neat golf lounge in an environment that inspires people to golf and socialize. We want to offer our visitiors an intimate experience with service as a compliment to the best possible golf experience indoors. RUFF is a place that gathers golfers of all levels. A place where new and established relationships grow out of the common interest for golf.
