HOW TO BECOME A MEMBER

Sign up for a membership

To become a member of RUFF, you need to visit one of our facilities. Membership is paid on the spot and you get instant access to all the benefits. Our memberships are signed annually and are valid for 1 year from the registration date.

The membership can be used at all facilities in Sweden.
