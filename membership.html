The Golf Stop Membership

With our QR technology that you will scan with your phone at the door for your tee times, you now can play all day, and all night. For members, The Golf Stop is open 24/7 at your convenience. the fast and easy way to shave strokes of your game (without breaking the bank). Get unlimited golf for you and up to 3 others per 2 hour session. With 4 membership options you can chose what suits you best.

Every new membership comes with a swag pack including a shirt golf balls and snack bar coupon.
MEMBERSHIP RULES
  • Membership ID is a generated QR code that will show on your phone.
  • Cancel anytime (not prorated)
  • Member must be present for any tee time booked
  • 10% discount is applied to any membership paid in advance 12 months.
  • 2 hours time allowed booked in advance
  • Members can bring ANY guest as long as the member is present (maximum 4 players)
  • 2 hours can be reserved at anytime, together or individually
    • Exception: if one hour is booked, there must be two hours between individual one hour bookings.
  • A new reservation can be made 45 minutes after the first reservation started (this will allow for extended stay if another bay is open)
    • EXAMPLE: a member can make a reservation on Monday at 9:00 am for 60 minutes and another reservation on Thursday at 9:00 am for 60 minutes (two 9 hole rounds for two or more people or 18 holes for single player)

    OR

    • EXAMPLE: a member can make a reservation for 18 holes by booking consecutive times (9:00 am & 10:00 am)
  • A $50 penalty could be applied to 3 or more missed appointments per month.
Regular Membership $229 Per month
  • Unlimited 2 hours of golf for you and up to 3 other guests
Youth Membership (17 and below) $99 Per month
  • unlimited 2 hours of golf for you and up to 3 other guests
Night Only Membership (10pm – 6am) $99 Per month
  • Unlimited 2 hours of golf between designated hours for you and up to 3 other guests
  • 50% off regular 2 hourrate once a month (Example: book a 2 hour time slot for $50 once each month)
  • Member will be able to schedule tee times only between the hours of 10:00 pm - 6:00 am (a 6:00 am tee time is only good for one hour ending at 7:00 am) (if a member wants an early 18 holes, a 5:00 am tee time is recommended)
Corporate Membership (3 User ID’s) $399 Per month
  • unlimited 2 hours of golf for you and up to 3 other guests
  • Corporate membership can reserve two bays together for 2 hours once each month (This applies per corporate membership, not per corporate user)
  • Membership rules still apply per membership, not per corporate user. One corporate user will not be able to book a tee time until 45 minutes into another users 2 hour time slot. If only one hour is booked by a user, another member or the same member can book another hour if it is not within 2 hours of each other.

With any questions, please reach out to support@thegolfstop.com for a quick response

HOW TO BECOME A MEMBER

Sign up for a membership

To become a member of The Golf Stop, you need to visit one of our facilities. Membership is paid on the spot and you get instant access to all the benefits. Our memberships are signed annually and are valid for 1 year from the registration date.

The membership can be used at all facilities in Sweden.
